# Design Notes

## Color Palette (Sporty Theme)

- **Primary**: `#FF6B35` (Vibrant Orange) - Energy, action, athleticism
- **Secondary**: `#00D9FF` (Electric Blue) - Speed, technology, dynamism
- **Accent**: `#FFD60A` (Bright Yellow) - Victory, energy, highlights
- **Background**: `#0A0E27` (Deep Navy) - Athletic, professional base
- **Surface**: `#151B3D` (Dark Blue) - Cards and elevated elements
- **Surface Highlight**: `#1E2749` (Lighter Blue) - Hover states
- **Text Main**: `#FFFFFF` (White) - High contrast readability
- **Text Muted**: `#94A3B8` (Slate 400) - Secondary information

## Typography

- **Font**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Weights**: 300 (Light), 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (SemiBold), 700 (Bold), 800 (ExtraBold).

## Animations

- **Blob**: Floating background gradients with organic movement
- **Pulse Glow**: Pulsing shadow effect on primary buttons
- **Slide Up**: Elements fade in and slide up on load
- **Hover**: Cards lift and glow on hover

## Glassmorphism

- Used on Header and Feature Cards.
- `backdrop-blur-lg` or `backdrop-blur-sm` with semi-transparent backgrounds.
- Thin borders (`border-white/5` or `border-white/10`) to define edges.

## Sporty Elements

- Diagonal accent stripes for dynamic energy
- Sports pattern background (subtle)
- Bold, action-oriented copy
- Stats bars with vibrant colors
- High-contrast color scheme for visibility
